I'm a Digital Media graduate student working with creative coding tools — p5.js, Max/MSP/Jitter, TouchDesigner, Arduino — to create browser-based experiences and interactive installations.

My practice explores the attention economy, digital mysticism, and how we relate to technology in increasingly mediated worlds. When I'm not debugging Max patches, I'm making digital art in Procreate, producing electronic music, or hunting for CRT televisions.
